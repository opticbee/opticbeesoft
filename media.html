Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our media and entertainment solutions

The best approach to launching an OTT platform depends on your content library, target audience, and business model. Generally, we recommend starting with a minimum viable product (MVP) that includes core functionality like content delivery, basic user management, and one primary monetization model. This allows you to test the market and gather user feedback before investing in more advanced features. Key considerations include content rights, technology stack, device support, and marketing strategy.

Development timelines vary based on project complexity, but a typical custom media solution takes between 4-9 months from discovery to launch. Simple OTT platforms or CMS implementations might be completed in 3-4 months, while more complex ecosystems with multiple integrated systems, custom features, and advanced analytics can take 6-12 months. We use agile methodologies that allow for phased releases, so you can start seeing value early in the process.

The most effective monetization model depends on your content, audience, and market position. SVOD (subscription) works well for services with extensive libraries and recurring value. TVOD (transactional) is ideal for new releases or premium content. AVOD (advertising-supported) can help reach wider audiences and generate revenue from free tiers. Many successful services use hybrid models—combining subscription with advertising or transactions—to maximize revenue potential. We recommend A/B testing different approaches to find what works best for your specific situation.

We implement multi-layered content protection strategies including DRM (Digital Rights Management) systems, tokenized authentication, watermarking, and secure video players. For live content, we use token-restricted streams and secure ingest points. We also monitor distribution channels for unauthorized content and implement technical measures to prevent recording and redistribution. The specific approach depends on your content value and risk profile, with options ranging from standard encryption to forensic watermarking and advanced anti-piracy services.

Yes, we specialize in integrating new solutions with existing systems. Our team has experience connecting with various CMS platforms, CRM systems, payment gateways, analytics tools, and content delivery networks. We use API-first approaches and middleware when necessary to ensure seamless data flow between systems. During the discovery phase, we thoroughly audit your current tech stack to identify integration points and potential challenges, ensuring a smooth implementation that enhances rather than disrupts your existing workflows.

We offer comprehensive support and maintenance packages tailored to your needs. These typically include 24/7 monitoring, regular software updates, security patches, performance optimization, and technical support. We also provide analytics reviews and strategic recommendations to help you maximize the value of your solution. Our support plans are flexible, ranging from basic maintenance to fully managed services where we handle everything from content operations to platform improvements. We believe in building long-term partnerships rather than just delivering projects.

We architect solutions with scalability in mind, using cloud infrastructure that can automatically scale to handle traffic spikes during major events. This includes content delivery networks with global reach, load-balanced streaming origins, and elastic transcoding capacity. We conduct load testing before major events to identify potential bottlenecks and implement caching strategies, connection management, and quality adjustment mechanisms to maintain performance under heavy load. For extremely large events, we can implement specialized solutions like multicast streaming or edge computing approaches.

We implement comprehensive analytics that track both business and user experience metrics. Key metrics include concurrent viewers, completion rates, engagement time, buffering ratio, bitrate quality, audience demographics, content performance, and revenue metrics. We also track user journey analytics to understand how people discover and interact with content. All data is presented in customizable dashboards that provide actionable insights for content strategy, technical optimization, and business decisions. We can integrate with your existing analytics tools or implement specialized media analytics solutions.
