Technology Case Studies

Real-world examples of how our technology integration solutions have transformed businesses

Global Retail System Integration

A multinational retailer with disconnected systems across 12 countries

The Challenge

Inventory discrepancies, inconsistent customer experiences, and inability to share data between regional systems were costing an estimated $4.2M annually in lost sales and operational inefficiencies.

The Solution

We designed and implemented a unified commerce platform with centralized product information management, real-time inventory synchronization, and a global customer data hub.

Results: 32% reduction in stockouts, 19% increase in online sales

Healthcare Data Integration

A regional hospital network with siloed patient data across 7 systems

The Challenge

Medical staff spent up to 2 hours daily navigating between systems to access complete patient records, leading to delayed treatments and potential safety risks.

The Solution

We developed a FHIR-based interoperability platform that unified patient records from EHR, lab, pharmacy, and billing systems into a single comprehensive view.

Results: 67% faster access to patient data, 41% reduction in administrative time

Manufacturing Automation

An automotive parts manufacturer with manual processes between departments

The Challenge

Manual data entry between design, procurement, production, and shipping systems resulted in a 12% error rate and production delays averaging 3 days per order.

The Solution

We implemented an IIoT integration platform that connected machinery, ERP, and supply chain systems with automated workflows and real-time data exchange.

Results: 89% reduction in data errors, 22% faster order fulfillment

Financial Services API Ecosystem

A financial institution needing to modernize legacy systems for digital banking

The Challenge

Outdated core banking systems couldn't support mobile banking, third-party integrations, or real-time data access, putting them at a competitive disadvantage.

The Solution

We created a microservices-based API layer that abstracted legacy systems, enabling secure integration with mobile apps, fintech partners, and data analytics platforms.

Results: 14 new digital services launched, 35% reduction in integration costs

Integration Projects

Our specialized integration solutions designed to address specific business challenges

CRM-ERP Integration

Seamlessly connect customer relationship management and enterprise resource planning systems to create a unified view of customer interactions, orders, and fulfillment processes. Our integration ensures data consistency across sales, marketing, and operations teams.

Payment Gateway Integration

Secure, reliable integration with multiple payment providers including Stripe, PayPal, and traditional banking systems. We implement tokenization, recurring billing, and fraud detection while ensuring PCI compliance and seamless checkout experiences.

IoT Integrations

Connect IoT devices and sensors to business applications for real-time monitoring, predictive maintenance, and operational intelligence. Our solutions include edge computing integration, data streaming, and automated response workflows.

Data Warehouse Integration

ETL/ELT solutions that consolidate data from multiple sources into centralized data warehouses or lakes. We design schemas, implement transformation pipelines, and ensure data quality for accurate reporting and analytics.

Chatbot & AI Integration

Integrate conversational AI and chatbot platforms with backend systems for customer service, IT support, and internal workflows. Our solutions include natural language processing, sentiment analysis, and seamless handoff to human agents.

Identity & Access Management

Single sign-on (SSO) and identity federation across multiple applications and services. We integrate with Active Directory, LDAP, OAuth providers, and multi-factor authentication systems for secure, convenient access management.
