Frequently Asked Questions

What response time can I expect for critical issues?

For critical issues (P1 incidents affecting business operations), we guarantee an initial response within 15 minutes and continuous effort until resolution. Our SLA defines specific response times based on issue severity, ensuring appropriate attention to all problems.

Do you provide 24/7 support coverage?

Yes, we offer round-the-clock support with our follow-the-sun model and dedicated night teams. Our support engineers are available 24/7/365 to address urgent issues, with full coverage during holidays and weekends.

How do you handle security updates and patches?

We follow a structured patch management process that includes evaluating patches for criticality, testing in non-production environments, scheduling deployments during maintenance windows, and validating successful implementation. Emergency security patches are expedited based on threat severity.

Can you support our custom-built applications?

Absolutely. Our team includes experts in various technologies and frameworks. We begin with a knowledge transfer process to understand your custom applications, then provide ongoing support, troubleshooting, and enhancement services tailored to your specific needs.

What reporting will I receive on support activities?

We provide comprehensive monthly reports including incident metrics (volume, resolution times, etc.), system performance data, security status, completed maintenance activities, and recommendations for improvement. Custom reports can be developed based on your specific requirements.
